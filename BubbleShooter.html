Bubble Shooter
Puzzle Bobble?
Taito Corporation of Tokyo, Japan is credited with having created the first "bubble shooter" game, releasing it under the name "Puzzle Bobble" in 1994. "Puzzle Bobble" was based on a game they had previously made called "Bubble Bobble," although it did not share the same format as the Bubble Shooter we all know today. It was in 2002 that the term "Bubble Shooter" was coined by Absolutist LTD, whose game featured the super-saturated colors associated with the genre as a whole. Although my first experience was on a flip phone, the game was transferred onto smartphones in 2010.
